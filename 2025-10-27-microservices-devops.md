**Class Notes – Microservices & DevOps (Today’s Session)**

* Microservices are small, independent services that can be deployed separately
* REST APIs are used for communication between services
* Stateless services do not store data internally
* External logging helps with easier error monitoring and debugging
* Load balancers manage and distribute incoming traffic
* DNS converts service names into IP addresses
* DevOps focuses on automation and collaboration between teams
* CI/CD enables faster and more reliable deployments
* Hands-on practice is essential to fully understand these concepts
